Privacy Policy

Last updated June 8, 2026

The short version

  • No account, no sign-up. We never ask for your name or email to use Cravings.
  • Your taste profile lives in your browser — on your device, not on our servers.
  • Location is used once, with your permission, to find places near you. We don't store it.
  • We don't sell your data, and there are no ads. Ever.

Cravings ("Cravings," "we," "us") is a taste-first food discovery app. You swipe on dishes, the app learns what you're in the mood for, and it points you to nearby places that serve it. This policy explains what the app does and doesn't do with information while you use it at themshin.com/cravings.

01No account, no profile

Cravings has no sign-up and no log-in. We don't create an account for you, and we don't collect a name, email, phone number, or password — because there isn't one. The only time we'd ever receive your email is if you choose to write to us at the address below.

02Your taste model

Every swipe nudges six taste dimensions — spice, sweetness, richness, sauce, veggies, and dairy — to build a picture of your palate. This taste model is stored locally in your browser (using local storage on your device). It is not tied to your identity and is not uploaded to a server we control.

Because it lives on your device, your taste profile stays with that browser. Clear your browser's site data and the model resets — there's nothing for us to delete on our end.

03Location

When you ask Cravings to find places nearby, your browser will request permission to share your location. If you allow it, we use your approximate location at that moment to look up restaurants around you. We use it to answer your request and then discard it — we don't build a location history or track where you go.

You can decline the permission, or revoke it anytime in your browser settings. The app still works; it just can't rank places by distance without it.

04Restaurants & maps

Place names, addresses, ratings, and map links come from third-party mapping and places providers. When you tap to open directions, you leave Cravings and hand off to that provider (for example, your device's Maps app), which is governed by its own privacy policy, not this one.

05Analytics

We may collect basic, aggregate usage signals — like how many sessions happen or which features are used — to understand whether the app is working. These are not tied to your identity and are not used to build an advertising profile of you.

06No ads, no selling data

Cravings shows no advertising and runs no third-party ad trackers. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone.

07Local storage, not tracking cookies

The app uses your browser's local storage to remember your taste model and a few preferences between visits. It does not use third-party advertising cookies. Clearing site data for themshin.com removes everything Cravings has saved on your device.

08Children

Cravings is a general-audience food app and is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children.

09Changes to this policy

If we update how the app handles information, we'll revise this page and move the "last updated" date above. Meaningful changes will be reflected here before they take effect.

10Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at hi@cravings.app.
